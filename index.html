About the program

Did you know that millions of women around the world lack access to basic hygiene products? This can have serious consequences on a woman's physical and emotional health, including increased risk of infections, embarrassment, and shame. That's why we're working tirelessly to provide essential care packages to women in need. With your help, we can make a difference in the lives of these women and give them the dignity they deserve.

At Empower and Elevate PGH Women, we provide essential personal hygiene kits that are distributed through the Greater Pittsburgh Foodbank and other local nonprofit partnerships. These kits help women in the area feel seen, supported and dignified.
